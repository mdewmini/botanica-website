Care Guide

How to Keep Your Plants Thriving

Follow our easy 6-step routine for healthy, happy houseplants:

  1. Choose the right spot — Match each plant to its ideal light level (bright indirect, low, or direct sun).
  2. Water correctly — Check soil moisture before watering; most plants prefer drying out slightly between waterings.
  3. Ensure good drainage — Always use pots with drainage holes to prevent root rot.
  4. Feed monthly — Use a balanced liquid fertiliser during the growing season (March – October).
  5. Mist & humidify — Tropical plants love humidity; mist leaves or place a pebble tray with water nearby.
  6. Repot when needed — When roots escape drainage holes, move your plant up one pot size in fresh soil.
